I have to say, recently I’ve been listening to two songs. One’s brand new, and the other one’s definitely an oldie for someone my age.

The first song is Beautiful Things, reminds me of a few others I’ve heard before, like Perfect. It’s got similar lyrics about finding your true love. Some kind of “After fulfilling some small desires, life feels more appreciated.” What’s something new to keep me on repeat with this song? Singers’s powerful voices, a bit of grit, using some vocal tricks with pitch changes…. I’m into songs that switch up the beat, going from calm to intense, which, is criticized by some people that said it’s too jarring, but I kinda dig it.

The second song, oh, it’s by Avril Lavigne! It’s Complicated! This song really uplifts me during these gloomy times.
